Saipan's Must Try Restaurants

Food is one of the bare necessities for our survival. However, if you truly want to live, you must have good food. So, in this article I'd like to list a few restaurants that I'd recommend for good eating.

My Top Picks:

  1. Himawari
  2. Shirley's Coffee Shop
  3. Mariana Lighthouse

Himawari | Garapan, Saipan

Himawari serves Japanese cuisine, which in my opinion is one of the best food cultures. The meals are fresh and light, and they offer a wide variety of choices ranging from sushi to bento boxes.

My Recommendations

  • Salmon Sashimi
  • Sea Urchin Nigiri
  • Porkloin Cutlet
  • Salmon Butter Saute

Shirley's | Susupe, Saipan

Shirley's is best known for it's amazing fried rice, one of my personal favorites being their Ground Beef Fried Rice. The menu is very diversified, guaranteeing a dish for every craving. Not to mention the serving sizes are great, often times one dish can feed 2-3 people.

My Recommendations

  • Breaded Mahi Mahi Fish Fillet
  • Ground Beef Fried Rice
  • Chamorro Sausage Scramble
  • Seafood Carbonara

Mariana Lighthouse | Garapan, Saipan

The once abandoned Mariana Lighthouse has now been restored and turned into a beautiful resaurant with a beautiful view. Along with the amazing view, customers can even enjoy a nighttime performance from dancers and a live band. Mariana Lighthouse even has a photgrapher that will take your photo, which you can then have printed out and framed right on the spot.

My Recommendations

  • BBQ Platter
  • Loco Moco
  • Chicken Sesame Salad
  • Spicy Tuna Roll
